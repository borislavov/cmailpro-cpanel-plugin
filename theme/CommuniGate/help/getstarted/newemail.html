What is the Getting Started Wizard?

The Getting Started Wizard can help you learn more about web hosting. It can also help you set up features in cPanel.

What is an email account?

An email account allows you to send and receive electronic messages. Each email account has a specific login (user@example.com) and a password. Each account also has a quota which limits how much disk space the account can use.

Note: Be sure to enable SMTP authentication in your email client, or you may not be able to send mail.

Adding email accounts

To add an email account, you will need to provide a login name in the Email field, a password in the 2 Password fields, and a quota.

Login name: Please note that you can add an email account for a subdomain, parked domain, or addon domain on your account by selecting that domain from the drop-down list.

Password: We recommend using a strong password for all email accounts, to prevent malicious users from gaining access to the accounts. cPanel can help by generating a strong password for you — just click Password Generator.

Quota: Setting a quota is a good idea, as all email takes up space, especially email with attachments. This space will count against your overall disk space quota.

After you provide the required information, click Create.
