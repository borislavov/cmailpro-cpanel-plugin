What is the Getting Started Wizard?

The Getting Started Wizard can help you learn more about web hosting. It can also help you set up features in cPanel.

  • To skip this step, click the Skip this step button at the bottom of the screen, or choose a step from the Jump to section drop-down menu.

What is a style?

A style is the appearance of an interface within a web browser. You can choose between several different styles on the cPanel interface.

  • Simply click a style picture to preview it.

Change your cPanel interface style

  • Click Apply in the preview window.

OR

  • Click Switch Style to below the style you want.
