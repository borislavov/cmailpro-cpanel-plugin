What is the Getting Started Wizard?

The Getting Started Wizard can help you learn more about web hosting. It can also help you set up features in cPanel.

Update your contact information

Please keep your contact information up-to-date in order to continue receiving important notifications.

1. Enter your primary and secondary email addresses in the appropriate boxes. These email addresses will function as the main contact points for notifying you of new information regarding your website.

Note: Please use email addresses that are not affiliated with your website. This way, if your website email is not operational, you will still be able to receive notifications.

2. Click Save.

3. Review the settings to make sure they’re correct.

4. Click Next Step.
