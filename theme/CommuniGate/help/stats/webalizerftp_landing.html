What is a The Webalizer?

The Webalizer is a complex program that displays various statistics for your website using tables and graphs.

Full documentation for “The Webalizer” can be found at its creators’ website here.

What is Webalizer FTP?

This feature allows you to view Webalizer statistics about users who access your site via FTP.

  • Click the link to the right of the magnifying glass icon to view statistics for your domain.
