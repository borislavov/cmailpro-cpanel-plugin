What are log programs?

Log programs allow you to see a visual representation of the visitors to your site. Using different log/statistics programs, you can see who is visiting your site, what they are viewing, what they are downloading, where they enter your site, where they exit, and more.

Configure log programs

1. Click the box under each log program you would like to have record statistics about your site.

2. Click Save.

Note: If a lock icon appears under a program, your web host does not allow you to use the program.
