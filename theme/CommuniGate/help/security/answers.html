Security Policy

What is the purpose of creating security questions and answers?

Answering security questions identifies you as the legitimate cPanel account owner when you log into your account from an unfamiliar IP address.

Review security questions and answers

To review your security questions and answers:

  1. Check your answers. If necessary, make a note of the answers you have set. You will need them later.
  2. To change the answers, click Click here to change answers or, to save them, click Save Answers.
