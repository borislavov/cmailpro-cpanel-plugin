What is a certificate signing request (CSR)?

A CSR is a request which you send to a certificate authority asking them to grant you an identity certificate.

cPanel can generate a CSR for you, but since authorities vary with regard to the information they require, you should check your authority’s requirements before applying for a certificate.

Generate a new certificate signing request

1. Select the appropriate host.

2. Enter your country, state, city, company, company division, email, and passphrase in the appropriate boxes.

3. Click Generate.
