Manage SSL certificate sharing

If you are the owner of the server, you can let your users share your SSL certificate.

Enable or disable sharing

  • To enable or disable sharing, click the Enable Sharing or Disable Sharing button.

Certificate linking

Your users can share your SSL certificate by directing visitors to a subdomain of your certified domain.

To see an example of how they should construct the HTML link, choose a user’s domain from the drop-down menu.
