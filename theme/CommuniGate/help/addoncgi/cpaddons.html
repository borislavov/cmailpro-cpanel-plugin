What are cPAddons?

cPAddons are pieces of software that you can install on your website through cPanel. cPAddons provide useful tools for your website, such as blogs, bulletin boards, chat, shopping carts, and guest books.

Enable or disable cPAddon update notices

You can choose whether to receive notifications when an installed addon has an available update. To do so:

  • Click the here link.

    • Note: Updating cPAddons helps prevent security issues.

    Install a cPAddon

    1. Click the link for the addon you wish to install.
    2. Read the License Terms.
    3. Type a password in the Password box. You will need this password to manage the addon in the future.
    4. Click Install.

    View additional cPAddon information

    1. Click the link for the addon whose information you wish to view. You will be directed to a new page.
    2. Click the available informational links.
