What are raw access logs?

Raw access logs are text files that contain information about your visitors and the content that they accessed on your site. The logs do not use graphics, such as graphs and charts.

Configure logs

  • To archive your raw access logs into the home directory every 24 hours, click the corresponding box and click the Save button.
  • To remove the archived raw access logs from the home directory at the end of each month, click the corresponding box and click the Save button.

Note: Archiving your raw access logs will count towards your disk space quota.

Download current raw access logs

1. Click a domain link in the Domain chart and the domain’s current raw access log will automatically download to your computer.

2. Use your preferred text editor to open the file.

Download archived raw access logs

1. Click the appropriate domain link in the Domain chart and the domain’s archived raw access log will automatically download to your computer.

2. Use your preferred text editor to open the file.
