Redirect a subdomain

This screen allows you to redirect a subdomain’s visitors. You can send them to a different area of your main site, or to a different domain.

1. Type the URL to which you wish to redirect the subdomain’s visitors.
2. Click Save.

Disable redirection of a subdomain

You can disable redirection, sending subdomain visitors to the subdomain’s original location. (The original location is the home folder you specified when setting up the subdomain. This directory appears under the Document Root heading of the table.)

  • Click Disable Redirection.
