This feature allows you to update your contact information.

Update Contact Information

1. Enter your primary and secondary email addresses in the appropriate boxes. These email addresses will function as the main contact points for notifying you of new information regarding your website.

Note: Please use email addresses that are not affiliated with your site so that, if your website email is not operational, you will still be able to receive notifications.

2. There are 3 different notification types available. Click the box to the right of each notification type you would like included in future emails.

3. Click Save.
