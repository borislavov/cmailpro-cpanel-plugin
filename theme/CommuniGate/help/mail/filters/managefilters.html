What is a filter?

A filter is a tool that processes mail according to your preferences. For example, a filter can automatically discard spam or save mail from a specified sender in its own folder.

In cPanel, filters can be applied to all email for the domain (Account Level Filters), or to each individual account (User Level Filters).

Manage filters by user

On this screen, you can manage filters for each email account at your domain.

  • To search for a specific account, type a keyword in the Search box and click Go.
  • Click Manage Filters to create or edit a filter for an email account.
