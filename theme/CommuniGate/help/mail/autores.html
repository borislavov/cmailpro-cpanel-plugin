What is an autoresponder?

An autoresponder allows you to send automatic responses to email messages. For example, if you are unavailable, you can let senders know when you will return or how to contact you while you are away.

Change managed domain

1. Click the Managing pull-down menu and select the appropriate domain.

2. Click Go.

Search autoresponders

1. Enter keyword(s) in the Search field.

2. Click Go.

Add an autoresponder

  • Click Add Autoresponder. A new page will appear.

Edit an autoresponder

  • Click Edit to the right of the appropriate email in the Current Autoresponders table. A new page will appear.

Delete an autoresponder

  • Click Delete to the right of the appropriate email in the Current Autoresponders table. A new page will appear.
