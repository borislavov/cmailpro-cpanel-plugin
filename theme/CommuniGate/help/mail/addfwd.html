Add a new email account forwarder

1. Enter the email address to forward in the Address to Forward field.

2. Click the domain pull-down menu and select the appropriate domain.

3. Select the appropriate destination (more options are available when you click Advanced Options):

  • To forward to an email address, enter the email address in the Forward to email address field.
  • To discard the email and send a failure message to the sender, select the appropriate option and enter the message into the Failure Message field.
  • To forward to the system account, enter the account’s cPanel username in the Forward to a system account field.
  • To forward to a program, enter a path in the Pipe to a Program field. Make sure your script uses the proper file permissions and you are using the correct hashbang.
  • To discard emails without notifying the sender, click Discard.

Note: We do not recommend that you use this option, because the sender will be unaware that the delivery failed.

4. Click Add Forwarder.
