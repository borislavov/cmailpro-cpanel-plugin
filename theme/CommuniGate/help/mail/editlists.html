Edit BoxTrapper list

Here, you can select a BoxTrapper list to modify. BoxTrapper’s lists contain the email addresses of people who send you mail.

  • Click the link corresponding to the list you wish to modify.

The available lists are:

  • Blacklist — A list of email addresses from which incoming mail will be automatically blocked. BoxTrapper sends a notification to correspondents who are blacklisted.
  • Ignore List — A list of email addresses from which incoming mail will be blocked. BoxTrapper does not send a warning message upon receipt of mail from an ignored address.
  • Whitelist — A list of email addresses from which incoming mail will automatically be accepted.
