Add or edit an autoresponder

1. Click the Character Set pull-down menu and select the appropriate character set.

2. Enter the interval, in hours, for the autoresponder to wait between responses to the same email address.

3. Enter your email address in the Email field.

4. Click the domain pull-down menu and select the appropriate domain.

5. Enter your name in the From field.

6. Enter your message subject in the Subject field.

7. Click the HTML Message box if you are including HTML in your message.

8. Enter your message in the Body field.

Note: In your message, you can include tags that will automatically insert information about the received email:

subject — The subject of the message that was sent to the autoresponder.

from — The name of the sender of the message received by the autoresponder (if the sender’s name was not received, from will replace it with the sender’s email address).

email — The sender’s email address.

9. Click Create/Modify.
