What is a BoxTrapper queue?

The BoxTrapper queue allows you to view any email waiting for verification. This can improve email receipt times, since you can manually view and approve messages.

Search BoxTrapper queue

1. Click the Search In pull-down menu and click the appropriate email section.

2. Enter keyword(s) in the for search string box.

3. Click Submit.

Navigate through BoxTrapper queue

  • Click << to view a previous date, >> to view a future date, or Return to current date to view the current date.

BoxTrapper queue actions

1. Click the box under the correct date.

2. Click the Delete or Whitelist & Deliver circle.

3. Click Submit.

View email

  • Click the email link.
