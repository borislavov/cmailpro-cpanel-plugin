Edit BoxTrapper configuration settings

1. Enter email addresses, separated by commas, that forward messages to your account in the Email addresses going to this account field.

2. Enter your name in the Your Name field.

3. Enter how many days that unverified logs and messages will be held in your queue in the Number of days to keep logs and messages in queue field.

4. Click the Enable Automatic White-listing option to enable automatic whitelisting. When automatic whitelisting is enabled, email addresses to which you send mail are automatically added to your whitelist.

Note: Enabling automatic white-listing does not affect how BoxTrapper treats mail from senders to whom you have sent mail in the past. These addresses will be white-listed regardless of whether you enable or disable automatic white-listing here.

5. Click Save.
